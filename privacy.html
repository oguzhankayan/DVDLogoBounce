Corner · Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 May 2026

Corner does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Full stop.

The short version

Corner is a screensaver app for Apple TV. It works entirely on your device. There is no account, no sign-in, no analytics or tracking SDKs, no advertising, and the app makes no network requests. Nothing about you or your usage ever leaves your Apple TV.

Information we collect

None. We do not collect any personal information, device identifiers, contacts, location, or usage analytics.

Information stored on your device

Corner saves the following locally on your Apple TV so the app remembers your setup between sessions. This data stays on the device, is not transmitted anywhere, and is removed when you delete the app:

  • Your preferences — the chosen theme, the word shown on the bouncing logo, and all customization settings (speed, size, colours, display mode, effects, etc.).
  • Your in-app statistics — counts such as "perfect corners", wall bounces, session streaks, and run time. These are anonymous numbers; they are never associated with an identity and never leave the device. You can reset them at any time in the app's Statistics screen.

Third parties

Corner contains no third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking code. We do not sell or share data because we do not have any. Your purchase is handled by Apple under Apple's own privacy policy; we do not receive your payment information.

Children

Corner is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page and shipped with the next app version. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.
