What it does

  • Your word on the disc. Type your name, your channel, anything — it rides the bouncing badge and re-tints on every wall bounce.
  • Six themes. Classic, Neon, Synthwave, Minimal White, Retro CRT, VHS — each restyles the logo, glow, background and particles. CRT and VHS add their own post-processing.
  • The corner hit, finally celebrated. A counter that never forgets, "time since last perfect corner", session streaks, a per-corner breakdown — plus an optional flash and screen pulse.
  • Make it yours. Speed, logo size, logo count, custom background colour, inter-logo collisions, and three display modes — Single, Multi, Chaos. Everything updates live.
  • Built for the couch. Minimal auto-hiding controls, a streamer/ambient mode, 60 fps, low-memory. Just leave it on.
  • Honest. Paid once. No subscription, no ads, no account, no tracking — your statistics never leave your Apple TV.
