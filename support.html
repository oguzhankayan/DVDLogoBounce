Frequently asked

How do I change the word on the disc?
On first launch, type it in the onboarding screen. Any time after that: press the Menu button on the Siri Remote → Customize → Logo.
How do I open the menu / change themes?
Press the Menu button on the Siri Remote while the screensaver is running. From there: Themes, Customize, Statistics, About. Play/Pause freezes the bounce; a swipe reveals the on-screen counter.
What counts as a "perfect corner"?
When the logo strikes a corner within a tight tolerance of both walls at once. Corner counts every one, shows the time since the last, tracks per-corner totals, and (optionally) celebrates it with a flash and a quick screen pulse.
Is there any sound?
No — Corner is silent by design. It's meant to be left running quietly.
Does Corner collect any data?
No. No account, no analytics, no network calls. Your statistics are stored only on your Apple TV.
Is it a subscription?
No. Corner is a one-time purchase — no subscription, no ads, no in-app purchases.
My screen looks dim / it stopped after a while.
Corner keeps the display awake while it's on the foreground. If tvOS still dims, check Settings → General → Screen Saver on your Apple TV, and make sure your TV's own power-saving isn't kicking in.
