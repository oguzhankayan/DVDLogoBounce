# Privacy Policy — Corner

_Last updated: 11 May 2026_

Hosted copy: <https://oguzhankayan.github.io/DVDLogoBounce/privacy.html>

---

**Corner does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**

## The short version

Corner is a screensaver app for Apple TV. It runs entirely on your device. There
is no account, no sign-in, no analytics or tracking, no advertising, and the app
makes no network requests. Nothing about you or your usage ever leaves your Apple
TV.

## Information we collect

**None.** We do not collect any personal information, device identifiers,
contacts, location, or usage analytics.

## Information stored on your device

Corner saves the following locally on your Apple TV so it remembers your setup
between sessions. This data stays on the device, is never transmitted anywhere,
and is removed when you delete the app:

- **Your preferences** — the chosen theme, the word shown on the bouncing logo,
  and all customization settings (speed, size, colours, display mode, effects,
  etc.).
- **Your in-app statistics** — anonymous counts such as "perfect corners", wall
  bounces, session streaks, and run time. They are never tied to an identity and
  never leave the device. You can reset them at any time in the app's Statistics
  screen.

## Third parties

Corner contains no third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking code. We do not
sell or share data because we do not have any. Your purchase is handled by Apple
under Apple's own privacy policy; we do not receive your payment information.

## Children

Corner is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including
children.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at the URL above
and shipped with the next app version. Continued use of the app after an update
means you accept the revised policy.
